About Us

Vijana Tech is a project that deals with capacity development to youth on various skill sets of Technology whereby we focus on awareness creation through organized training sessions, online training, mentorship programs and supply of opportuniies for the purpose of helping our beneficiaries scale up in their careers. We target youth with different backgrounds both in school and out of school who are passionate to learn on technology. We offer programs in Digital Marketing, Graphic Designing, Web Development, Software Development, Softwares and Hardwares to RPA.

Vijana Tech is an Innovation place where we look at some of the most pressing problems in our community and solve them in a technology perspective. We founded several side projects including which some will soon operate and have them on the go.

Vision

We aspire to see "Creative youth entrepreneurs in the field of technology who are proactive to venture in the digital economy and bring about digital transformation in Tanzania."

Mission

Our Mission is to create awareness about technology to youth through training programmes, linking them with opportunities to access e-resources for the purpose of learning modern technologies as well as mentorship programmes.

Core Values

Passion, Commitment, "One-team" Attitude, Excellence, Focus and Continuously Improve.

What We Do

Training.

We do Training on various skill sets of Technology whereby we focus on awareness creation through organized training sessions as well as online training. We offer programs in:

  • Web Development - How to build professional modern websites.
  • Computer Programming - How to build applications and software programs.
  • Hardwares & Softwares - How to install, maintain and roubleshoot Softwares & Hardwares.
  • Graphics Designing - How to create visual content.
  • Digital Marketing - How to market products or services on digital space.

We've trained over 100 students in our two trainings so far.

Software Development

We listen to your ideas, create, design, deploy and support custom software solutions from the ground up, designed especially for your business.

We develop websites, blogs, business sites, portfolios, online stores, mobile apps or anything else you can imagine.
